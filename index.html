Our top tours in Bogota and surroundings

In our portfolio of services, you can find everything from historical and cultural patrimony tours, running through the most interesting districts of Bogota, to nature getaways for you to discover the wildlife hidden by the cosmopolitan city. At Hansa Tours we offer a wide range of experiences designed for you to explore each destination according to your interests.

Monserrate tour in Bogota

This icon so beloved by Bogota citizens is easily recognizable by the immaculate white church built on the top of the hill, which can be appreciated from all points of the city. Its interior houses the sculpture of the Fallen Lord of Monserrate, Bogota's patron saint, which attracts hundreds of thousands of pilgrims each year seeking spiritual comfort.

Duration: 3 to 8 hoursDifficulty: LowSuggested departure time: 8:00 a.m.

Price per person in US dollars for a tour of 2 travelers. Discounts apply for more people.

Zipaquira Salt Cathedral tour

There are thousands of cathedrals, churches and chapels throughout the country, but there is only one that is 180 meters (590 feet) below the surface: the Salt Cathedral of Zipaquirá, listed as the first wonder of Colombia. If your stay in the capital is time-limited, we at Hansa Tours recommend you put this destination at the top of your list.

Duration: 5 to 10 hoursDifficulty: HighSuggested departure time: 9:00 a.m.

Price per person in US dollars for a tour of 2 travelers. Discounts apply for more people.

La Candelaria Bogota tour

Located among the imposing mountains of the Andes, Bogotá, the capital of Colombia, is a cosmopolitan city, full of secrets and stories to be discovered. To walk through the cobbled streets of downtown Bogotá is to be a spectator of a space where architecture, art and culture come together to tell the story of the place where the city was born.

Guatavita Lake tour

The Legend of El Dorado recounts a precolonial time when the indigenous Muisca priests submerged themselves in the sacred lake of Guatavita. Covered in gold dust, they conducted sacred ceremonies to pay tribute to the spirit of the water with gold artifacts forged and hammered by their most skilled artisans.

Duration: 6 to 10 hoursDifficulty: HighSuggested departure time: 8:00 a.m.

Price per person in US dollars for a tour of 2 travelers. Discounts apply for more people.

Bogota graffiti tour

The Historic Center, La Candelaria, Suba, Rafael Uribe Uribe and Santa Fe are just some of the locations that make Bogota the largest open-air gallery on the continent.

Bogota botanical garden tour
